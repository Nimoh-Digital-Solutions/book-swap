## Full Description

BookSwap connects book lovers in your neighbourhood for free book exchanges.
List a book you've finished, browse what others have available nearby, and
arrange a swap — no money changes hands, just great reads.

**How it works:**
- Scan a barcode or search to add books you want to share
- Browse a map of available books in your area
- Request a swap and propose one of your own books in return
- Chat with the other reader to arrange the handoff
- Rate your exchange and build trust in the community

**Features:**
- Barcode scanner for quick book listing
- Location-based discovery with interactive map
- Real-time messaging between swap partners
- Counter-offer system for flexible exchanges
- User ratings and reviews for trust
- Wishlist to track books you're looking for
- Push notifications for new requests, messages, and updates
- Dark mode and multilingual support (English, French, Dutch)
- Biometric login (Face ID / fingerprint)

BookSwap is free to use. No fees, no subscriptions — just books changing
hands between real people in your community.

## Google Play Data Safety Summary

**Data collected:**
- Email (account management)
- Name (profile, visible to other users)
- Approximate location (finding nearby books)
- Photos (book covers, profile avatar)
- Push notification token (notifications)
- Crash logs (Sentry, for stability)

**Data NOT collected:**
- Financial information
- Health data
- Browsing history
- Contacts / address book

**Security:**
- Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- Users can request account deletion (in-app settings)
- Data is not shared with third parties for advertising
